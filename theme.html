Synfig Studio 2D Animation Hackathon. The theme for animation is:



The Panchtantra is an ancient Sanskrit collection of stories. Team should create a 2D animation for these stories, using Synfig Studio software.

Please find the list of Panchatantra stories along the moral here.

You can have the stories interpreted according to the current situations happening across the world. For example the climate change, elections, pandemic situation, air pollution, etc. Please make sure that the moral of the story is not changed.

The participants have to submit their animation at https://courses.fossee.in. After confirming your participation in the Hackathon, your login credentials for submission will be generated and sent to you on the registered email ID.

Please note: An email is sent to your registered email ID, where you have to confirm your participation in this Hackathon. Please make sure that this form is filled only by the team lead.

Please find the Guidelines for submission here.

Please follow the steps given below to submit your animation.

  1. Go to https://courses.fossee.in
  2. Click on the "Login" button available on the top right of the page.
  3. Enter the username and Password as received over mail and click on "Log in".
  4. After logging in for the first time, you will be prompted to update the password.
  5. Click on the "Synfig Studio 2D Animation Hackathon" available in the left panel of the Welcome page.
  6. Scroll down and go to "Animation Submission Upload" section.

  7. Go to Click here to add your submission" to submit your files for the hackathon.
